PIAGETS — Parameter-Importance-weighted Attractor-Graph Embedding for Task Schematization. A continual learning system maintaining a library of attractor schemas via a compact 24-dim signature, deciding whether new time-series are assimilated into existing schemas or accommodated as new ones.

Quick start: A 10-task stream loads automatically. Open Model and press ▶ to begin. Ground-truth attractors appear in orange as each task starts; model free-runs in blue every 20 epochs.
